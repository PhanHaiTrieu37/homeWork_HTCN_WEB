One particularly highly recommended book is The Street Attorney by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins to reevaluate his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs in his law firm. So he gets acquainted with the city's streets , and recognizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in court. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. John Grisham's other books include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
